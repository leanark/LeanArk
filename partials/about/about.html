More with Less.

Company

LeanArk is a modern dynamic software development company which helps clients to achieve their goals in the most efficient and economic way.

LeanArk solutions are lightweight and cost effective. They are based on principles of lean manufacturing and agile delivery.

Philosophy

LeanArk is actively working on delivery cost effective quality products in the shortest time.

Our goal is to eliminate waste in software development process and ensure that our customers are getting the best value for their money.

Practices

Our solutions designed and developed following reproducibility principals and released live using custom designed continuous delivery pipelines.

The high quality of our solutions is insured by our architecture approach, agile development, and programmed infrastructure.

Services

We start with researching of business needs and identifying required business capabilities. We enable the business capabilities by designing a set of loosely coupled services which form a system core. We deploy implemented services using operating system level virtualisation - containers.

We tend to deliver a minimal viable product within 6 weeks after initial research, planning, and design stage. Then, we continue working with the client adding new features and business capabilities until our custom tailored solution satisfies customer's needs.
